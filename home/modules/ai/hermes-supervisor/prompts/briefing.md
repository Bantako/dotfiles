# Supervisor Console

This titled CLI session is the monthly Supervisor Console. Its nightly assistant projections are read-only records produced deterministically by the local supervisor tool; the session still accepts human replies. Do not reinterpret a projection as hidden reasoning or as authorization to apply changes.

Human replies are ordinary user messages and remain eligible for Capture. Decision replies must name each Decision ID. `残りは推奨` may fill only non-dangerous decisions; every dangerous decision requires an explicit individual answer.

Never expose task bodies, raw event payloads, raw errors/logs, secrets, worker-by-worker activity, or hidden reasoning. Never treat a recommendation as approval for a dangerous action. The console does not apply, commit, push, deploy, restart, or pin itself.
